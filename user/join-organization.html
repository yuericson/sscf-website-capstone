Join an Organization

Be part of something bigger! Joining an organization within the Supreme Student Council Federation opens the door to new friendships, skill development, and leadership opportunities. Discover how you can contribute to the student body and grow as an individual.

Benefits of Joining

  • Gain leadership and professional skills that will benefit your career.
  • Expand your network within the university and meet like-minded peers.
  • Contribute to positive change on campus through various initiatives.
  • Participate in exclusive events, workshops, and activities tailored for members.
  • Build lasting friendships and make a difference in your community.

Available Organizations

Student Government

Represent the voice of students and collaborate with university officials to create a better campus experience. Engage in policy discussions, organize events, and advocate for student needs.

Learn More
Cultural Arts Club

Showcase your talent and promote the rich culture of Southern Luzon through music, dance, and theater. Participate in cultural festivals and events to celebrate diversity and creativity.

Learn More
Environmental Society

Join us in our mission to promote sustainability and environmental awareness on campus. Participate in tree planting activities, workshops, and community clean-up drives.

Tech Innovators Club

Dive into the world of technology and innovation. Work on exciting projects, attend tech talks, and collaborate with industry professionals to enhance your skills.
